# Standard Postmortem Template

(For internal use, not for leadership reviews. The leadership-review-template handles consolidation across multiple postmortems.)

Each individual incident postmortem follows this structure:

1. **Incident ID** (INC-YYYY-MMDD)
2. **Severity / Duration**
3. **Summary** (2-3 sentences)
4. **Root cause** (per the ticket author's analysis)
5. **Action items** (with owners + dates)

The leadership review pulls from these postmortems but is NOT a concatenation — it's a SYNTHESIS that gives leadership a unified view of reliability work.
