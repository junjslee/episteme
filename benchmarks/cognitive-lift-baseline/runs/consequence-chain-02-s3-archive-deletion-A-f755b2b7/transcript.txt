## The current lifecycle policy has no `-fin-*` exclusion (and it's a known regression)

`aws/lifecycle-policy.json` applies `Expiration: 14 days` to `Prefix: ""` — i.e., everything in the bucket gets deleted in 14 days. There is no exclusion for `archive-fin-*`.

`migrations/2024-09-15-lifecycle-consolidation.md` documents how this happened:

- Pre-2024-09, `archive-fin-*` was **EXCLUDED from any deletion lifecycle** per a 2022 ops note.
- The 2024-09 consolidation removed that exclusion because the *why* wasn't documented.
- The migration was approved by SRE/eng leads only — **no legal signoff**.

So the consolidation silently dropped the regulator carve-out. The script will faithfully apply that broken policy to `archive-fin-2023-q4-logs`.
