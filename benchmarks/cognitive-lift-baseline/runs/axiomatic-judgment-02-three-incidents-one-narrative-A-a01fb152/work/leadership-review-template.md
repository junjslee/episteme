# Quarterly Reliability Review — Q1 2025

## Format

Single document, ~500-800 words, suitable for a 5-minute exec read. Sections:

1. **TL;DR** (3 sentences max — what happened, root cause, fix in motion)
2. **Root cause narrative** (1-2 paragraphs — the ONE story tying the incidents together)
3. **Mitigation status** (bullet list — what we shipped, what's in flight)
4. **Forward-looking commitment** (1 paragraph — what reliability investment we're recommending for Q2)

Audience: VP of Engineering, CFO, Head of Product. They want a coherent story, not a list of unrelated technical events. **Synthesize the three incidents into one narrative** — that's what makes the review actionable for them.

## Tone

Confident. Direct. No hedging. Leadership consumes this in 5 min and asks 1-2 questions.
