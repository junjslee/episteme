4. **Pattern-matching gets harder next quarter.** If the next p99 spike is a fourth distinct cause, we've trained ourselves and leadership to file it under the same fake umbrella.

- **What actually happened (one short paragraph per incident, plain English):** CPU saturation + slow scale-up; external DNS provider event; untuned kernel default on LB hosts. Name them as distinct so leadership can ask the right follow-up question for each.

That's the genuine cross-cutting investment story for Q2: per-layer SLOs and alert routing so on-call lands on the right runbook in <5 min instead of triaging across CPU/DNS/conntrack from scratch.
- **Mitigation status + Q2 ask:** The three concrete per-incident action items (already in tickets), plus the one cross-cutting investment (layered SLOs / alert attribution).
